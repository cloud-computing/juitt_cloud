== Welcome to Juitt

We believe that people should be able to easily find good movies online. We believe that friends want to share the good movies they've seen with others and that is way Juitt is here.
Juitt allows you to follow other people with your same taste on movies and be able to download a good movie with only one click. No more spending time searching for good movies in torrent trackers or other search tools and downloading fake movies.
Juitt will change the way people consume movies online.
